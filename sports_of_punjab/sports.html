Sports of Punjab

Kabaddi

Kabaddi is the traditional and most popular sport of Punjab. It showcases strength, strategy, and stamina, played both locally and internationally.

Wrestling (Kushti)

Wrestling, known as Kushti, is a traditional sport often held in akharas. Punjab has produced several national and international level wrestlers.

Field Hockey

Punjab has a rich legacy in field hockey. It has produced many players who represented India at the Olympic level, making it a proud sport of the state.

Shooting

Punjab has a strong presence in shooting sports. Many young shooters from the region have represented India in international competitions.

Weightlifting

The strength and endurance of Punjabi youth are evident in weightlifting, with many making it to national and international championships.

Cycling

Cycling events and races are commonly held in Punjab. The sport is gaining popularity for both competition and fitness.

About Punjab

Punjab, the land of five rivers, is a vibrant blend of traditions, history, and hospitality. Known for its iconic Golden Temple, it symbolizes peace and spirituality.
